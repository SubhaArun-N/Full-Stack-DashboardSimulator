Zoho Integration Guidelines
  • Goto https://accounts.zoho.com/developerconsole and add a Client id by filling necessary details.
  • From the Zoho developer console , copy and paste your client ID and client secret.
  • The url should be the complete url of your website . Example: https://yourwebsitename.com
  • Dont include '/' at the end of your website.
  • To receive the grant token,Click on the icon near the grant token.
  • You will be redirected to a page.Copy the URL and paste it in the grant token text field.
  • Click on Save to complete the Integration.
